Travel

I have been to many places around the world, but one of my favorite places to go to is Greece. Mostly all of my family lives there, so me and my parents go there every summer to see them.

Martial Arts

I first started karate when I was just 5 years old, and I earned my black belt when I was 16. Aside from self defense, karate has taught me that anything is possible if you put in enough time and effort to reach your goals.

Music

I first started learning to play the clarinet all the way back in 3rd grade. I stuck with it until my junior year in high school when I switched over to percussion. I am currently in the Huskie Marching Band at NIU as member of the drumline and enjoy every minute of it.
